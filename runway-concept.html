RUNWAY CONCEPT

I made this model of a runway as a response to the overproduction of clothing in the fashion industry, a lot of which never reaches the hands of the consumer. In my runway concept, the model walks out of a clothing store and directly into the mountain of textile trash. The sides of the walls are plastered with fashion advertisements to demonstrate how people are bombarded with advertisements despite the fact that a great deal of it ends up unused and even unsold. The bleachers directly face the runway to evoke a feeling of helplessness but also to some degree, personal responsibility.

Year: 2020
Independent art project.
